A crow is a type of bird in the crow family.
The crows are mostly black, with short and strong legs and massive beaks. They are omnivores, but they prefer meat, and especially scum.
A crow can be a dangerous animal, while another(human or another animal) will be near its nest it may threaten and attack. In addition, crows are intelligent and possessive animals that are attracted to glittering objects. This fact may also result in an assault.
