A rooster, also known as a cockerel or cock, is a male gallinaceous bird, with cockerel being younger and rooster being an adult male chicken.
Crowing is one of the clearest signs of being a rooster.
A rooster, is a domesticated chicken, the most common of the domesticated animals and of all the birds on earth.
The rooster are held by man for the purpose of producing meat and feathers.
The rooster are also held by man for the purpose of conducting cockfights and sometimes also as pets.
The rooster is not a dangerous to man, but if he is harassed he may attack.
